How to Play

Rules


The Pirate Game is an online multiplayer activity where each person in the game, on their own mobile device, places a set of symbols into a [typically] 6x6 blank grid. The grid is labelled A-F and 1-6 down. Therefore, you could, for example, place 'shield' in the square with reference A6, a '£1000 Note' in C4 etc. The host will then spin the spinner to move to the next round the game will add this to their 'money' and this is kept as a running total throughout the game. The square that has been chosen by the spinner will be indicated on your grid and the game will act according to the item placed within it. For example, a £1000 note will increase your cash while the 'kill' item will allow you to chose a an unfortunate target whose money will be reset to 0. The winner is the player at the end of the activity that has the highest amount of money when both their 'bank' and their ongoing money are combined.

The Symbols


Symbol Definition
Steal

- This allows the player to 'rob' someone else's money

Dagger

- This symbol allows the player to wipe out another player’s money returning them to 0!
Gift

- This allows the player to give a 'present' of £1000 to another player of your choice. This money is not deducted from your own balance.
Swap

- This allows the player to swap their money with another person's balance.
Shield

- This symbol allows the player to 'block' any incoming action against them. Once collected from the grid it can be used at any time - but only once.
Mirror

- Allows a player to reverse any incoming action against the player that initiated it. This means that they fell the consequences of the action instead of you. Once collected from the grid it may be used at any time - but only once. If a mirror is used against you, you can not then use a mirror or shield against that. Swap actions may not be mirrored as this would still be swap.
Bomb

- Your own money is reset to 0. This may be neither shielded nor mirrored at any point.
Bank

- All of the player's money is placed in the bank. When in the bank the money is invulnerable - it may not be altered by either another player's actions or by bombs.
